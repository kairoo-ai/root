My call

B14d (9.5) and A8d (9.5) are the strongest — both read "K" instantly while staying all-curves. B14d keeps your exact original arc and adds a lower leg; A8d is the cleaner one-stroke take. B8c and B3d are great middle-grounds if you want it lighter.
